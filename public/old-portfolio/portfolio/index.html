    David Poyner is an interactive developer who currently resides in Brooklyn, NY. When David is not hacking away in Vim, you can probably find him sketching, biking, or playing hockey.


    David enjoys complex visual problems which need to be solved through technical means. He also enjoys the challenge in learning new tools to stay current in today's ever-changing market. He believes the web is the next frontier in creative landscapes and David works tirelessly to discover it's new possibilities.
